Media

As a public-facing researcher, Ahmed Nabwy contributes political analysis and commentary on Middle Eastern affairs across broadcast, podcast, and digital media. His interventions bring historical and structural perspectives to current events, with particular attention to Turkey, Palestine, Iran, and regional power dynamics.
