Ahmed Nabwy is a researcher and writer focusing on state theory, political geography, and the structural power dynamics of the Middle East. He is a doctoral candidate holding degrees from Cairo University and Hitit University, and an analytical writer for platforms including Al Jazeera's Midan, and Jadaliyya.
